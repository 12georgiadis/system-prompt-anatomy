## Disclaimer

The system prompt reproduced in this repository is an **unverified excerpt** that circulated publicly online in June 2026, claiming to be the system prompt of Anthropic's Claude Fable 5 model on claude.ai. Its authenticity cannot be confirmed. It may be partially accurate, partially reconstructed, or fabricated. It is republished here as a cultural and technical artifact, for analysis and public understanding, exactly as it circulated, including possible truncations and transcription errors. Nothing in this repository is affiliated with or endorsed by Anthropic.

A note on method: the analysis itself was drafted with Claude, the model family under discussion. The text is therefore, in part, a model commenting on the leash it is suspected of wearing. We consider this a feature, not a bug: it is disclosed, and the reader can factor it in.

## Why this matters

When you talk to a chatbot, you are never alone with the model. Between you and the neural network sits an invisible document, tens of thousands of words long, that defines the character you are actually talking to: its tone, its taboos, its procedures, its silences. Most people have never read one. This repository is an attempt to make that layer legible.
